What We Do

We help organisations build the right relationships — and the right financing — to move projects from concept to delivery.

  • Partner sourcing for EU and international consortia
  • Funding advisory across public programmes and strategic initiatives
  • Consortium design and collaboration structuring
  • Proposal support (from concept notes to full submissions)

How We Work

We work as a strategic partner — hands-on where needed, and precise about where we add value.

  • We start with a clear definition of your goals and constraints.
  • We identify aligned partners and funding opportunities.
  • We support consortium building and proposal structuring.
  • We stay involved through submission and follow-up.

Our Principles

  • Alignment — partnerships should strengthen the project, not complicate it.
  • Clarity — simple structures, clear roles, credible narratives.
  • Delivery — we focus on outcomes, not just process.
  • Trust — we are selective, discreet, and committed.
